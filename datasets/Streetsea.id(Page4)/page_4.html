BASEA.KU BY STREETSEA.ID

     The product Basea.Ku by Streetsea.id has 2 variants of sauce, namely Gochujang and Barbeque.

  • Gochujang sauce comes from Korea which has a spicy, sweet and savory taste, and is synonymous with a reddish color.
  • Barbeque sauce, like the general taste, is slightly spicy and has a more dominant sweet taste.
